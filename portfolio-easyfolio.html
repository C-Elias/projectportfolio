EasyFolio - Cryptocurrency management solution

EasyFolio is an intuitive cryptocurrency management solution that both assists in managing your portfolio and discovering new opportunities. We all know what cryptocurrency is, but how do we properly manage an investment portfolio of various coins, sometimes in different exchanges, as well as analyze new opportunities.Creating a wallet in your Dashboard allows you to connect with the coins that you hold on different exchanges, giving you insights in an easy-to-understand format. All you have to do is add your coins and the amount you own of each.
